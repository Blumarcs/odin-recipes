Chocolate Chip Cookie

Soft and Rich Chocolate Chip Cookie

Ingredients

  • l cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbs vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups + 2bsp flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips

This is a very straight forward and easy recipe. You will be ready to bake and eat this delicious cookie within no time at all.
